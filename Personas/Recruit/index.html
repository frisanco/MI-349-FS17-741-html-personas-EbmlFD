Decisions are made quickly. His childish tendencies under pressure cause him to jump to conclusions. Therefore, the main page of the site MUST spark his interest.

Facts or Emotion?

Montana bases his decisions purely on emotion, and so follows his work ethic.

He does work very well when he is willing and interested on a particular topic, but quickly declines if motivation does not present itself.
