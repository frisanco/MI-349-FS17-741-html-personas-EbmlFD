Name

Gary Mitchell

Descriptor

Current Earth Beyond client

Quote

"We are preparing a sales pitch to our engineering team. Can you supply a project summary for your proposed excursion to Earth's moon?"

"Funding will not be an issue if we can persuade them to jump on board."

Who is it?

Age 40; Stern but fair; wants to see success for Earth Beyond.

Title: Lead collaborative engineer; works with teams from various organizations to ensure collaborative success and productivity.

What Goals?

The main goal is profit. Gary wants the best from each team in order to reach a "best" collaborative solution.

Gary demands respect, hard work, and CONSTANT communication in regards both to successes and failures.

What Attitude?

Expects professionalism, courtesy, but also some leeway in regard to strictness.

Appearance should be welcoming, but should not appear childish.

Which Behavior?

When visiting websites, shops, etc., Gary tends to get a feel for what the company is about. He'll skim through the vairous pages to gauge his interest on whether or not this company is worth the effort of partnering with. Overused graphics, animations, or improperly formatted text will immediately turn Gary away.

Trends

Professional website and shop architecture is a quality that Gary vigorously seeks.

Functional Importance

Gary does not express much emotion, nor does he seek to have it sparked while researching a company. He emphasizes the company's appearance should be professional and organized.

Decison Making

If presented properly, Gary will make a thorough evaluation of the company's shop/website ideas and design. Low professionalism quickly turns Gary away.

Facts or Emotion?

Gary makes most decisions based on fact; companies that have a disorganized site or appearance will not thrive.
